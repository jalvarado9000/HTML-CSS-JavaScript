About this Dog

The Golden Retriever is a large-size breed of dog bred as gun dogs to retrieve shot waterfowl such as ducks and upland game birds hunting and shooting parties and were named 'retriver' because of their ability to retrieve shot game undamaged. Golden Retrievers have a instinctive lover of water and are easy to train to basic or advance obedience standars.
